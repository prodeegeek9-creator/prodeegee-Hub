Our Story

We believe great teams deserve great tools — not a dozen apps duct-taped together. Prodeegee Hub is the workspace we always wished we had.

Our Mission

One workspace.
Zero friction.

Prodeegee Hub was born from a simple frustration: modern teams were spending more time managing tools than doing actual work. Switching between project trackers, chat apps, email clients, and calendars was killing productivity.

We set out to build a single, beautifully designed workspace that brings projects, tasks, team communication, email, calendars, and analytics together — with real-time collaboration baked in from day one.

Today, thousands of teams across startups, agencies, and enterprises rely on Prodeegee Hub to plan smarter, move faster, and deliver better results together.

What We Stand For

Our values

The principles that shape every decision we make — from product design to customer support.

Simplicity First

We ruthlessly remove complexity. Every feature earns its place by making teams more productive, not just adding to the feature count.

Teams Over Individuals

The best work happens when teams are aligned. We build every feature to improve visibility, reduce silos, and keep everyone on the same page.

Speed Matters

Slow tools kill momentum. We obsess over performance — from page load times to the speed of Supabase real-time updates in your inbox.

Privacy by Design

Your team's data is yours. We build with security and privacy as foundations, not afterthoughts. No selling data, ever.

Customer Obsession

Every piece of feedback makes the product better. We listen, we ship, we iterate — and we treat support as a product feature.

Transparency

We publish our roadmap, communicate changes clearly, and give teams the visibility they need to plan around our platform confidently.

Solutions

Built for every kind of team

Whether you're a two-person startup or a 500-person enterprise, Prodeegee Hub adapts to how you work.

For Startups

Move fast without breaking things. Get your whole team aligned from day one with shared projects, real-time chat, and visual task tracking — all free for small teams.

For Agencies

Manage multiple clients and projects without juggling tools. Separate workspaces, resource management, and client-ready reporting keep every engagement organized.

For Enterprise

Enterprise-grade security, SSO, custom roles, and priority support. Prodeegee Hub scales with you and meets your compliance requirements out of the box.
